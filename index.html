Our Story

"The secret of great seafood is simplicity and freshness."

At Matamkom, we take pride in being your primary destination for local seafood lovers. We started with a simple vision: to bring the freshest catch of the day directly to your table with the authentic taste you know and love.

We believe in quality over everything. Whether you're dining in with your family or ordering online for a cozy night, we guarantee a professional service and a taste that reflects the true essence of Egyptian coastal cooking.
